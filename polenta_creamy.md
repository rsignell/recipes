# Creamy Polenta Instant Pot
## Ingredients:
* 1 cup polenta (e.g. bob's red mill)
* 3 cups of water or broth
* 1/2 t salt
* 3 T butter
* 1/2 cup milk or half and half
* 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
## Instructions
* spray instant pot liner with PAM
* combine polenta and water or broth in instant pot
* cook on high pressure for 10 min
* 10 min natural release
* whisk for 30s
* add butter, half & half and cheese
