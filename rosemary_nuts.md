# Nigella Lawson Union Square Bar Nuts
## Ingredients
* 500g pecans
* 8 g fresh rosemary leaves (without stems)
* 8 g dark brown sugar
* 6 g salt (I use regular table salt)
* 16 g butter
* 1 g cayenne pepper
## Instructions
* Roast the nuts at 350F for 10 min
* Finely chop the rosemary
* Microwave the butter, sugar and salt in a large glass bowl for 1.5 min
* Add the cayenne and rosemary and mix well
* Add the hot nuts and mix well until the nuts are cool enough to eat
